NFPA 1582 Standard

Why Annual Physicals Matter

NFPA 1582 establishes the standard for comprehensive occupational medical programs for fire departments. Compliance means thorough evaluations designed specifically for the demands of firefighting.

Early Detection

Catch cardiovascular, pulmonary, and musculoskeletal issues before they become emergencies.

Reduce LODD

Departments with annual physicals see significantly fewer line-of-duty cardiac events.

Legal Compliance

Meet OSHA and NFPA standards while reducing department liability and insurance costs.
