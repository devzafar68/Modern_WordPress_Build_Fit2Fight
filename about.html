Our Mission

Protecting Those Who Protect Us

At Fit2Fight, our mission is simple yet vital: ensure every first responder has access to comprehensive, NFPA 1582-compliant wellness exams that can detect health risks early and keep them fit for duty.

Cardiovascular events remain the leading cause of line-of-duty deaths among firefighters. Regular wellness screenings can identify risk factors before they become life-threatening.

Every firefighter deserves the same quality of care they provide to their communities.

Our Team

Expert Medical Professionals

Our medical team consists of board-certified physicians, registered nurses, and certified medical technicians who specialize in occupational health for first responders.

Each team member is trained in NFPA 1582 standards and understands the unique physical demands and health risks associated with firefighting.
